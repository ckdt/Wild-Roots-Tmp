This is our manifesto

First: the party is ALWAYS in the kitchen.
Second: get off the internet and GO OUTSIDE.

We believe in small wardrobes and howling wolves. Clothes you can wear untill they fall apart. Fair trade. We also believe that Wu Tang will forever be for the children. We believe in distortion pedals but also in wah-wah. Getting dirty. We disagree with borders. Bash ‘em. We believe in The Beastie Boys. We love strange fruit. Speaking up. Cutting off sleeves. We like to make noise. Good vibrations. Wax on, wax off. We love climbing trees. We don’t care about making mistakes. We believe in DIY. Making waves. Messing up. Falling and getting our asses back up. We believe in writing letters, not texts. Video days and Neneh Cherry. Boys that rather be girls. Girls that rather be dinosaurs. We believe in magic, but hate clowns. We believe in a love supreme and sleazy speed metal. Using windows as doors. Peanutbutter milkshakes. Come as you are. Painting the wrong fence. We are outsiders. Outside is free. We are Wild Roots.
